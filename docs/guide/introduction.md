# Introduction to SOLPREDS

SOLPREDS is a decentralized prediction market that allows you to bet on short-term price movements of assets like SOL/USD.

## What is a Prediction Market?

A prediction market lets users speculate on future events by buying and selling outcome shares. In SOLPREDS, you predict whether the price will go **UP** or **DOWN** during a specific time window.

## Key Concepts

**Round**: A betting period (e.g., 5 minutes) where users predict price direction

**Lock Price**: The price when betting closes

**Close Price**: The final price that determines winners

**Payout Multiplier**: Your potential winnings based on pool distribution

**Prize Pool**: Total SOL bet in the round (minus fees)
