    Welcome to SOLPREDS

    Predict anything. Win everything. On-chain.

    SOLPREDS is a lightning-fast prediction market built on Solana. Bet on SOL price movements, earn rewards, and compete with traders worldwide.

    Get Started{ .md-button .md-button--primary } API Docs{ .md-button }

    ‚ö° Features

    - **Lightning Fast** Built on Solana for near-instant transactions and low fees - **Simple & Fair** Just UP or DOWN. Winner-take-all pool system - **Non-Custodial** Your wallet, your keys. All bets on-chain - **Real-Time Data** Powered by Pyth Network oracle

    üéØ How It Works

    1. Connect Wallet - Use Phantom, Backpack, or any Solana wallet
    2. Choose Direction - Bet UP or DOWN on the next 5-minute round
    3. Watch Price - Round locks, price captured from Pyth oracle
    4. Claim Winnings - Winners split the pool proportionally
