## Features

- **Lightning Fast** - Built on Solana for near-instant transactions and low fees
- **Simple & Fair** - Just UP or DOWN. Winner-take-all pool system
- **Non-Custodial** - Your wallet, your keys. All bets on-chain
- **Real-Time Data** - Powered by Pyth Network oracle

---

## How It Works

1. **Connect Wallet** - Use Phantom, Backpack, or any Solana wallet
2. **Choose Direction** - Bet UP or DOWN on the next 5-minute round
3. **Win Rewards** - Correct predictions split the entire pool
4. **Track Stats** - View your performance and climb the leaderboard
